1·le MONUMENTA DE INSULA MANNIAE.

JOHN BRADSHAWE TO COLONEL DUCKENFIELD.

A.D. 1651.

STATE PAPER Omen.

R,—The bearer John Christian, being a native of thais island,
bath informed us of diverse thing-s of good concernment to
the State, Whereof he can also give you notice. WVee desire you
to receive his informatione, and make such use of them on the
States behalfe as you shall see cause.
Signed, See„
J o. Bradshawe.
Whitehall, 23 Deeemb., 1051.
